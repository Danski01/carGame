Welcome my Dear!


The game "Car Intelligence" is a very simple single-player game which is acting as a great entertaining platform for TRUE car's LOVERS.
Game Rules
  • Be attentive
  • Try to guess the letters of which a random car's brand is consisted of
  • Do not loose all of the main 8 car parts

Game Concept
Your goal is to guess all the letters from a randomly generated car brand correctly to win the game. Initially, you will be given 8 lives according to the idea of 8 main details of a car without which car is disfunctional.
Be 100% attentive during the game process, because a player is provided with the list of already guessed words as an additional help.
Good luck with the game!
